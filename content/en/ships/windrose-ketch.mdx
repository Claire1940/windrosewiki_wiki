## Initial Repairs and Crew Recruitment

Before you can take the wheel of a ketch, you must first locate the derelict hull and perform essential field repairs. Unlike smaller boats, a ship of this magnitude cannot be operated by a single person effectively. Your first major objective involves gathering enough supplies to get the vessel floating and then scouting for a capable crew to man the rigging.

The repair process is resource-intensive. You should prioritize clearing nearby islands of basic materials before attempting to launch. Once the ship is floating, your immediate focus shifts to the "Shipwright’s Workshop" questline, which unlocks the ability to install heavy weaponry and defensive plating.

| Repair Phase | Required Action | Key Resource |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hull Integrity** | Patching leaks and reinforcing timber | **Hardwood & Fiber** |
| **Launch Prep** | Clearing debris and greasing the slipway | **Animal Fat / Oil** |
| **Crewing** | Rescuing sailors from pirate camps | **Rations & Gold** |

> **Warning:** You cannot finish the repairs and get the ship back to sea with just two people. Ensure you have completed the "Rescued Sailors" quest before attempting to set sail.

## Building the Shipwright's Workshop

The Shipwright's Workshop is the heart of your naval operations. However, simply placing the blueprint is not enough. In Windrose, advanced crafting stations require protection from the elements to function. You must construct a sturdy roof over the workshop area to unlock the crafting menu for ship components.

Furthermore, the workshop must be placed within the "Bonfire Range" of your camp. If you attempt to build it too far down the coast, the structure will remain inactive. Once the roof is secured and the station is powered by your central campfire, you gain access to high-tier naval upgrades like 12-pounder cannons and hull bracings.

### Workshop Construction Requirements

To build a functional workshop in 2026, you will need the following materials:

*   **Wood:** 40-60 units for the base frame.
*   **Copper:** 5-10 units for specialized tools.
*   **Thatch/Wood for Roofing:** Sufficient amount to cover the entire footprint of the station.

## Resource Farming: Copper and Wood

To fully outfit the **windrose ketch**, you will need a steady supply of copper. Copper is not as abundant as stone or wood and often requires venturing into dangerous territory. Look for "X marks the spot" locations on your map, as these often lead to caves or hidden grottos where copper veins are plentiful.

One of the most efficient ways to farm copper is to locate the cave systems guarded by the "Drowned." While these enemies are resilient, the copper yield in these zones is significantly higher than surface nodes. Always bring a shovel and a pickaxe, as some resources are buried beneath the sand or embedded in rocky outcrops.

| Resource | Primary Location | Usage for Ketch |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Copper Ore** | Jungle Caves / X-Marks | **12-Pounders & Bracing** |
| **Sturdy Timber** | Inland Forests | **Hull Repairs & Masts** |
| **Cloth Fiber** | Coastal Shrubbery | **Sails & Boarding Gear** |
| **Pearls** | Deep Water / Rare Drops | **Trading & Artifacts** |

## Upgrading Your Ketch for Combat

Once your workshop is operational, you can begin the "Manage Ship" phase. The **windrose ketch** has several slots for customization. In the current 2026 version of the game, players should focus on a balanced build that emphasizes both durability and range.

1.  **12-Pounder Cannons:** These are your primary source of damage. They require a significant amount of copper but are essential for taking down Blackbeard’s lieutenants.
2.  **Hull Bracings:** These increase your ship's health pool and reduce the damage taken from enemy broadsides.
3.  **Boarding Party Gear:** While boarding is high-risk, having the right gear allows your crew to seize enemy vessels for extra loot.
4.  **Repair Kits:** Always keep a "Black Flagship" or similar repair item in your active arsenal. This allows for mid-battle hull restoration, which can be the difference between victory and a watery grave.

> **💡 Tip:** If you find yourself struggling with boarding mechanics, focus entirely on your cannon accuracy. Circling the enemy and maintaining a medium distance is often safer than attempting to grapple.

## Naval Combat Strategies

Combat in the **windrose ketch** is a test of positioning and timing. The ship features an immersive crew system where your sailors will sing shanties while cruising, but they snap into action the moment a pirate ship is spotted. 

When engaging Blackbeard's forces, remember that you cannot leave the wheel while the ship is in high-speed motion. You must throttle down or steady your course before you can manually assist with cannons or repairs. Watch the wave height; heavy swells can cause your cannon shots to go wide, so time your broadsides for the crest of the wave.

### Fighting Blackbeard’s Lieutenants
The lieutenants often use artifacts to turn their men into "obedient undead." These ships are more aggressive and will attempt to ram your vessel. Use the superior speed of the ketch to stay out of their ramming arc while peppering their hull with 12-pounder fire. Defeating these captains will reward you with "Insignias," which are required to progress the main story and cure the "black marks" transformation affecting your character.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the Windrose ketch in the demo?**

A: You must follow the main questline until you reach the "Ship under your hand" objective. This requires gathering basic materials to repair the initial hull and finding a crew in the nearby jungle camps.

**Q: Why can't I use my Shipwright's Workshop?**

A: The workshop requires two conditions to function: it must be within the bonfire's heat range and it must have a roof built directly over it. If it is raining or the structure is exposed, the crafting menu will be locked.

**Q: Where is the best place to find copper for ship upgrades?**

A: The most reliable copper source is found in the cave systems marked on the third map of Blackbeard's crew. Be prepared to fight "Drowned" enemies who guard these mineral-rich veins.

**Q: Can I customize the appearance of my ketch?**

A: While the 2026 demo focuses on functional upgrades like cannons and bracings, the full game release promises expanded cosmetic options including sail patterns and figureheads. Currently, you can change the "Arsenal" items which slightly alters the ship's profile.

**Q: What happens if my ship sinks?**

A: If your ship's health reaches zero, you will respawn at your last bonfire. You will need to use resources to repair the hull back to a seaworthy state, so always keep a reserve of wood and copper in your camp storage.

## How to Set Up a Multiplayer Session

Getting your crew together in Windrose is a straightforward process, but there are a few nuances to the hosting system that you should be aware of. The game utilizes a "Captain's Instance" model, where the host’s world progress and base locations serve as the primary environment for the session.

### Steps to Invite Friends:
1. **Launch the Game:** Select "Play" from the main menu and choose your existing Captain profile.
2. **Host a Session:** Choose "Host Game" and set your privacy settings (Public, Friends Only, or Invite Only).
3. **Invite via Overlay:** Use your platform's social overlay (e.g., Steam Overlay) to send direct invites to your crew.
4. **Assign Roles:** Once your friends join, you can assign them permissions to build in your base or operate specific ship systems.

| **Shipwright** | Repairing hull damage during battle. | Support-focused players |
| **Quartermaster** | Managing loot and resource distribution. | Organized players |

## Building and Survival in Co-op

The building system in Windrose is arguably its strongest feature. In a multiplayer setting, this system allows for the creation of massive pirate fortresses that would be impossible for a solo player to maintain. Coordination is key here; while one player focuses on the structural integrity of the sea-facing walls, another can work on the interior crafting stations and defensive emplacements.

### Collaborative Building Tips:
* **Resource Pooling:** Create a central "Logistics Chest" where all players can dump raw materials like wood, iron, and hemp.
* **Blueprint Sharing:** Use the blueprint tool to "ghost" a building plan so your friends can see where you intend to place walls and towers before they commit resources.
* **Terraforming Safety:** As discovered in early playtests, using the shovel to dig large holes can be dangerous. Ensure your teammates aren't standing in the dig zone, as players can become trapped and perish if the terrain geometry shifts unexpectedly.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Windrose support cross-progression?**

A: Currently, your character's stats and inventory are tied to your specific account. While you can bring your character into different worlds, base progress is tied to the host's save file. The developers are investigating cloud-based cross-progression for a future update.

**Q: Can I play Windrose solo if my friends aren't online?**

A: Yes, Windrose is fully playable as a solo experience. The game scales enemy difficulty and ship management based on the number of players in the session. You can also hire AI crew members to help manage your ship when playing alone.

**Q: Is there PvP in Windrose multiplayer?**

A: As of the current 2026 build, the focus remains primarily on PvE (Player vs. Environment). However, there are specific "Contested Waters" zones where players can opt-in to combat against other crews for high-tier loot.

**Q: Will windrose crossplay be added to PlayStation and Xbox?**

A: The developers have expressed a strong interest in bringing Windrose to consoles with full **windrose crossplay** support. While no official release date has been set for the console ports, the architecture of the game is being updated to support controller inputs and cross-platform networking.

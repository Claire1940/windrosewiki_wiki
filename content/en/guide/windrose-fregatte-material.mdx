## Advanced Resource Gathering: The Duplication Method

For players who find the grind for **windrose fregatte material** too time-consuming, there is an advanced method involving save-file manipulation. This technique allows you to duplicate items by exploiting how the game saves character data versus world data. In Windrose, your character's inventory and your ship's stash are stored in separate files from the world itself. This allows you to "transfer" items between instances.

### How to Duplicate Materials

1.  **Locate your Save Files:** Open your Windows search bar and type `%appdata%`. Navigate back one folder to "Local," then find the **R5** folder. Go to `Saved > SaveProfiles > Worlds`.
2.  **Backup the World:** Identify the ID of the world containing your materials. Copy this folder and paste a backup on your desktop.
3.  **Extract Items:** Log into the game, enter the world, and move all the **windrose fregatte material** into your character’s personal stash.
4.  **Restore the World:** Log out of the world (you don't need to close the game). Delete the current world folder in the directory and replace it with your desktop backup.
5.  **Repeat:** When you log back in, your character still has the items, but the world has been restored to its previous state with the materials back in their original chests.

## Farming Locations for Rare Resources

If you prefer the "legit" way of playing, you'll need to know exactly where to find the rarest **windrose fregatte material**. The game world is divided into tiers; the materials for a Fregatte are generally found in Tier 4 and Tier 5 zones.

### The Whispering Woods (Ironwood)
The Whispering Woods is the primary source of Ironwood. You will need a Steel Axe or better to harvest these trees. Watch out for the Level 40 Ents that guard the groves; they are resistant to physical damage but vulnerable to fire.

### The Frost-Bitten Peaks (Hardened Steel)
To create Hardened Steel, you first need to mine Cobalt and Iron from the Peaks. These are then smelted together at a Blast Furnace. The environment is harsh, requiring cold-resistant gear or plenty of cooked meals to maintain your stamina.

### The Great Swamp (Reinforced Rope)
Vines found in the Great Swamp can be processed into Reinforced Rope. This is a vital **windrose fregatte material** for the rigging phase. The swamp is filled with poisonous gas pockets, so a gas mask or high resistance stats are mandatory.

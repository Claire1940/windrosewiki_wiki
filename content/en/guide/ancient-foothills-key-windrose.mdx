## Preparing for the Needle in a Haystack Quest

Before you set out to find the key, you must ensure your character is properly leveled and equipped. The Foothills region is unforgiving, and the quest "Needle in a Haystack" will push your combat skills to the limit both on land and at sea. We recommend a minimum level of 35 before attempting the naval portion of this quest.

| Requirement | Recommended Level/Item | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Character Level** | 35+ | Survival against high-level pirates and wolves. |
| **Ship Armor** | Reinforced Hull | To withstand "The Unsinkable" broadside attacks. |
| **Weaponry** | Parrying Dagger / Longsword | Essential for the final 1v1 boss encounter. |
| **Consumables** | Health Elixirs & Stamina Buffs | Necessary for the boss's high-pressure rush attacks. |

> **Warning:** The Foothills Temple Key is a legendary quest item. While it is indestructible and can stack up to 100, you cannot sell it or trade it to other players. Ensure you have adequate inventory space before starting the final leg of the quest.

## Phase 1: Clearing the Western Pirate Camp

The journey begins at the extreme western edge of the Foothills. Your first objective is to dismantle a localized pirate operation that holds intelligence regarding the temple's location. This camp is heavily guarded by sharpshooters and melee bruisers.

1.  **Infiltrate the Camp:** Approach from the southern cliffs to avoid the watchtower's line of sight.
2.  **Eliminate the Captain:** The camp leader drops a logbook that triggers the next phase of the quest.
3.  **Loot Resources:** Check the crates around the camp for high-tier cannonballs, which you will need for the upcoming naval battle.

Once the camp is cleared, the quest marker will update, signaling the arrival of a legendary ship on the eastern side of the map.

## Phase 2: Sinking "The Unsinkable"

The naval battle is perhaps the most cinematic part of the hunt for the **ancient foothills key windrose**. You must track down a massive vessel known as "The Unsinkable." Despite its name, the ship has a specific weakness to sustained broadside fire and circular maneuvering.

### Naval Combat Strategy

When you engage The Unsinkable, do not attempt to trade shots while stationary. The enemy ship has superior firepower and will quickly shred a static target. Instead, maintain a circular path around the vessel. This allows you to dodge incoming fire while keeping your own cannons aimed at their hull.

| Ship Component | Damage Priority | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Masts** | High | Reduces the ship's ability to turn and flee. |
| **Hull** | Critical | Necessary to trigger the boarding sequence. |
| **Deck Cannons** | Medium | Reduces the incoming projectile pressure. |

After depleting the ship's health, move in for the boarding action. You will need to clear the deck of remaining hostiles. Once the ship is secured, search the captain's quarters for the next clue, which points toward the northwestern ruins.

## Phase 3: The Northwestern Ruins and the Wax-Stained Desk

With the naval battle won, your next destination is the northwestern ruins. This area is infested with deadly wolves that can easily overwhelm an unprepared traveler. These wolves have a high bleed proc chance, so keep your distance or use area-of-effect (AoE) abilities to clear them quickly.

Navigate through the ruins until you find a small, makeshift camp. Inside, you are looking for a specific interactable object: the **Wax-Stained Desk**. Reading the documents on this desk will reveal the exact location of the pirate holding the key.

> **💡 Tip:** Use a torch or a light spell in the ruins. The wolves often hide in the shadows and will ambush you from the high ledges if you aren't paying attention.

## Phase 4: Finding the Ancient Foothills Key Windrose

Following the updated quest marker, head to the northern side of the Foothills. You will come across a final pirate camp. Unlike the previous encounters, this isn't a massive battle. The camp appears to have been recently raided by something else, leaving many of the pirates dead.

Search the pockets of the dead pirates scattered around the camp. One specific corpse, located near the northernmost tent, contains the **ancient foothills key windrose**. Once you have the key in your inventory, the "Needle in a Haystack" quest will update to its final objective: "Enter the Ancient Temple."

## Phase 5: The Temple Gate and Israel Hands Boss Fight

The Ancient Temple is located deep within the ruins. Approach the massive sealed stone door and interact with the mechanism to use your key. As the door grinds open, prepare yourself for a 1v1 duel against the boss, Israel Hands.

### Israel Hands Combat Mechanics

Israel Hands is a fast-paced boss who relies on rush attacks and projectile spam. He does not use a shield, meaning he is vulnerable to parries if your timing is precise.

| Move Name | Description | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shadow Rush** | A high-speed dash toward the player. | **Parry** at the moment of impact for a stun. |
| **Flintlock Fan** | Fires three projectiles in a cone shape. | Roll forward to get behind him. |
| **Blade Whirl** | A 360-degree spinning melee attack. | Backstep twice to stay out of range. |

The best strategy for defeating Israel Hands is to play defensively. Wait for him to finish a full combo sequence—usually three or four hits—and then strike while he is in his recovery animation. If you successfully parry his Shadow Rush, you can land a critical hit that deals massive damage.

## Rewards and Legendary Artifacts

Defeating Israel Hands is the final step in this arduous journey. Once the boss falls, you will be granted access to the inner sanctum of the temple.

The rewards for this quest are among the best in the mid-to-late game of Windrose. You will receive a powerful legendary sword with high critical strike chance and a unique artifact that boosts your naval combat stats.

1.  **Legendary Sword:** Features a "Bleed" passive that mimics the wolves of the ruins.
2.  **Ancient Artifact:** Increases cannon reload speed by 15%.
3.  **Experience:** A massive boost to your questing and combat levels.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the ancient foothills key windrose located?**

A: The key is found in the pocket of a dead pirate at a camp on the northern side of the Foothills. You must reach this stage of the "Needle in a Haystack" quest for the item to appear.

**Q: Can I skip the naval battle and go straight to the key?**

A: No. The quest is linear. You must clear the western camp, sink "The Unsinkable," and investigate the wax-stained desk before the key will spawn in the northern camp.

**Q: What happens if I lose the Foothills Temple Key?**

A: The key is a legendary quest item and is marked as indestructible. It cannot be dropped or destroyed while the quest is active. If you cannot find it in your inventory, check your "Quest Items" tab specifically.

**Q: Is Israel Hands a mandatory boss?**

A: Yes, if you wish to complete the "Needle in a Haystack" quest and gain access to the legendary artifact rewards, you must defeat him in the Ancient Temple.

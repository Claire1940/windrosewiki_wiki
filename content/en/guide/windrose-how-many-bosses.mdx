## Captain Thomas Richards: The Coastal Jungle Gatekeeper

The first major hurdle in your journey is Captain Thomas Richards. Located within the Coastal Jungle, Richards serves as a tutorial for the "dodge and retaliate" rhythm that defines Windrose combat. Before engaging him, ensure your gear is fully upgraded to level five.

### Strategy and Attack Patterns

The core strategy against Richards is a consistent "dodge to the right" maneuver. Most of his swings have wide arcs that favor his left side, making a rightward dodge the safest way to reposition for a counter-attack.

*   **The Triple Dodge:** Dodge right, right, and right again to clear his combo. Follow up with a light attack to chip away at his health.
*   **Heavy Attack Interruption:** If you time a heavy attack correctly, Richards will occasionally "flinch" or abort his swing. This is a high-risk, high-reward tactic.
*   **The Red Blink Grab:** Watch for the red visual indicator. This signifies a grab attack. Do not dodge too early; wait for the flash to ensure your invincibility frames cover the duration of the grab.

> **💡 Tip:** Carry at least 10 healing potions and 10 bandages for this fight. Use the "Retribution" talent to transform incoming damage into temporal health, which you can then recover by landing your own hits.

## Israel Hands: Mastering the Foothills Duel

Once you reach the Foothills, the difficulty spikes with Israel Hands. This boss has tighter opening windows and more complex combos than Thomas Richards. You will need to be more disciplined with your stamina management here.

### Tactical Breakdown

Israel Hands utilizes a mix of melee swipes and ranged gunplay. The key to this fight is baiting his charge and punishing his recovery frames.

| Attack Type | Player Reaction | Opening Window |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Double Swipe** | Dodge backward twice | Single Light Attack |
| **Bulb Summon** | Stay close, don't panic | Single Heavy Attack |
| **Red Charge** | Side dodge on red blink | Guaranteed Heavy Attack |
| **Aerial Jump** | Continuous movement | Bandage/Heal Window |

When Israel Hands zooms upward, he will attempt to shoot you from above. This is the perfect time to create distance and use a bandage. He may attempt to shoot his gun to interrupt your healing, but if you are at a sufficient distance, you can usually finish the animation before he re-engages.

## The Tainted Priestess: The Final Challenge

The Tainted Priestess is significantly more difficult than the previous two bosses. This fight is a "gimmick" encounter that requires the use of a ranged weapon. You should have your gear upgraded to level 15 and consider using consumables like the **Elixir of Concentration** or **Elixir of Cruelty** to boost your damage output.

### The Pustule Mechanic

The Priestess has glowing pustules located across her body. You cannot deal significant damage to her main health bar until these are burst. 

1.  **Ranged Necessity:** Use a **Plague Pistol** (for healing) or a **Reliable Musket** (for fast reloads).
2.  **Unfocus Strategy:** To hit the pustules more accurately, toggle off your target lock. This allows you to aim your swings and shots manually at the specific growths on her back and sides.
3.  **Stagger Window:** Once enough pustules are destroyed, she will enter a vulnerability phase. This is when you should unleash your highest damage heavy attacks.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Priestess has a "puke" attack and a "tongue slam" that deal massive area-of-effect damage. Always keep enough stamina for a triple dodge when she begins her charging animation.

## Optimizing Your Build for Windrose Bosses

When considering **windrose how many bosses** you can take on in a single session, your build efficiency is the deciding factor. A crit-focused build is currently the most effective way to burn through boss health bars.

### Recommended Gear and Talents

*   **Philli Buster Set:** Reduces stamina consumption for attacks, allowing for more frequent dodges.
*   **Conquestador Set:** Provides essential damage reduction for those who struggle with dodge timings.
*   **Sturdy Halberd:** Offers a 10% increase to critical hit chance.
*   **Planning Ahead Talent:** Increases passive gun reload speed while the weapon is holstered, which is vital for the Tainted Priestess fight.

### The "No-Strength" Strategy
Interestingly, high-level players often avoid investing points directly into Strength. Because stat scaling has diminishing returns after 20 points, it is more efficient to use **Meaty Croc Kebabs** to reach the 20-strength cap and spend your actual talent points on Mastery (for crits) and Vitality (for survival).

## FAQ

**Q: In Windrose how many bosses are currently in the game?**

A: There are currently three main progression bosses: Captain Thomas Richards, Israel Hands, and the Tainted Priestess. Defeating them is required to unlock new regions and high-tier ships like the Frig.

**Q: Do I need a gun for every boss fight?**

A: While not strictly required for the first two bosses, a ranged weapon is almost mandatory for the Tainted Priestess to safely burst the pustules on her body and trigger her stagger phase.

**Q: What is the best healing item for boss encounters?**

A: You should carry a mix of 10-15 healing potions for immediate health recovery and 10 bandages for use during long-range phases, such as when Israel Hands is in the air or the Priestess is repositioning.

**Q: Can I skip the bosses to reach the jungle?**

A: No, the bosses act as regional "keys." You must defeat Captain Thomas Richards to move from the Coastal Jungle to the Foothills, and the subsequent bosses to progress further into the game's map.

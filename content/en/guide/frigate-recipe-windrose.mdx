## Prerequisites for the Frigate Recipe

Before you can even think about laying the keel for a Frigate, you must satisfy several progression requirements. The game prevents players from "over-leveling" their way past challenges by gating essential crafting recipes behind boss encounters. Even if you have gathered the necessary ore, you cannot process it until you have proven your worth in combat.

### The Revenge Best Served Cold Quest
The hunt for the Frigate is tied directly to the "Revenge Best Served Cold" quest. This mission acts as the gateway to the mid-game content. You will be tasked with exploring the northern reaches of the map to gather "Black Marks." These marks are essentially keys required to enter the boss arena where the recipe's gatekeeper resides.

> **Warning:** Do not attempt to skip the exploration phase. The boss arena is "signposted" clearly, but you will be denied entry if your quest log does not reflect the collection of all required Black Marks.

## Defeating Thomas Richard: The Iron Gatekeeper

The most significant hurdle in obtaining the **frigate recipe windrose** players covet is the battle against Thomas Richard. This boss is notorious for his "input reading" mechanics and explosive attacks. Defeating him is the only way to unlock the smelting of Iron Ingots, which are the backbone of Frigate construction.

### Boss Attack Patterns
Thomas Richard is a tanky opponent who punishes greedy players. He utilizes a mix of melee combos and ranged explosives that can quickly deplete your health bar if you aren't careful with your stamina management.

*   **Bomb Spread:** Richard will wind up and toss several bombs across the arena. The spread can be unpredictable.
*   **The Grab:** A high-tracking move that deals massive damage. It is best avoided by dodging sideways rather than backward.
*   **Melee Combos:** He utilizes a 2-3 hit combo. Some versions of this attack are "red" (unblockable/heavy), while others can be parried.
*   **Pistol Dodge:** If you attempt to use a pistol at range, he will almost always dodge the shot immediately upon the trigger pull.

### Combat Strategy
The most effective way to wear him down is through a "hit and run" tactic. Wait for the end of his attack animation, move in for exactly two hits, and then dodge away. Avoid slow, heavy attack animations as he will likely recover and counter-attack before your swing connects.

## Optimizing Your Build for Shipbuilding Quests

To survive the journey toward the **frigate recipe windrose**, you need to invest your attribute points wisely. Endurance is the most critical stat during the mid-game transition. A larger stamina bar allows for more frequent dodges and longer sprints during the exploration phases of "Revenge Best Served Cold."

### Recommended Attribute Allocation
Focusing on the left-hand side of the skill tree provides the tankiness necessary to survive Richard's explosive combos.

| Attribute | Recommended Level | Key Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Endurance** | 12+ | Massive Stamina Pool |
| **Marathon Runner** | Maxed | Reduced Stamina Consumption |
| **Temporal Health** | Level 3 | Damage Recovery on Attack |
| **Stout Frame** | Level 2 | Increased Damage Mitigation |

> **Tip:** If you are struggling with the boss, ensure all your current equipment is at least Level 5. Additionally, cooking a high-quality meal before the fight can provide a temporary health buffer that might be the difference between victory and defeat.

## Crafting the Frigate: Materials and Costs

Once Thomas Richard is defeated and the **frigate recipe windrose** is accessible, you must gather the physical materials to begin construction at the shipyard. The Frigate requires significantly more resources than the Sloop or Schooner, specifically focusing on processed metals.

### Resource Breakdown
Iron Ingots are the primary bottleneck. Even though you can find Iron Ore in the southern regions early on, you can only convert them to Ingots after unlocking the smelting skill from the boss.

1.  **Iron Ingots (50x):** Smelted from Iron Ore found in southern fast travel points.
2.  **Hardwood Planks (100x):** Processed from Oak or Teak trees found in the central islands.
3.  **Heavy Canvas (20x):** Crafted from hemp and refined at a loom.
4.  **Reinforced Ropes (15x):** Requires high-tier fiber and resin.

For more information on resource locations, check the [Official Windrose Community Hub](https://steamcommunity.com/app/windrose) for updated map markers and community-sourced gathering nodes.

## Advanced Shipbuilding Tips

Building the ship is only the first step. To truly utilize the Frigate, you should look into specialized equipment that complements its slower, more methodical playstyle compared to smaller vessels.

*   **Two-Handed Mastery:** Since the Frigate is a heavy ship, leaning into a two-handed weapon build often fits the "heavy hitter" theme. Many players find that the pistol's mechanics are less effective against the armored enemies you'll face in Frigate-tier zones.
*   **Stamina Management:** Even with the ship's increased health, your character still needs high endurance to manage deck fires and repairs during combat.
*   **Healing Efficiency:** Use bandages for minor scrapes to save your high-tier potions for major naval sieges.

## FAQ

**Q: Where do I find the Black Marks for the frigate recipe windrose quest?**

A: Black Marks are located in the northern region of the map. You must clear out specific pirate encampments and explore hidden coves as part of the "Revenge Best Served Cold" questline. The quest log will track how many you have collected.

**Q: Can I get the Frigate without fighting Thomas Richard?**

A: No. Thomas Richard is a mandatory progression boss. He unlocks the ability to smelt Iron Ingots, which are a required component for the Frigate's construction. If you find the fight too difficult, consider lowering the world difficulty or upgrading all your gear to Level 5.

**Q: What is the best stat to level up first?**

A: Endurance is widely considered the best stat for early and mid-game. It increases your stamina bar, allowing you to dodge more frequently and use the "Marathon Runner" skill to its full potential during boss fights.

**Q: Is the pistol effective against Thomas Richard?**

A: Generally, no. The boss has an "input read" mechanic where he will automatically dodge as soon as the pistol is fired. A melee-focused strategy—hitting twice and then dodging back—is much more reliable for this encounter.

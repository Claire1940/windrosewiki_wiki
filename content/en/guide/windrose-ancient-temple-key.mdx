## Prerequisites: Starting the Needle in a Haystack Quest

Before you can even catch a glimpse of the **windrose ancient temple key**, you must be on the specific quest titled "Needle in a Haystack." In Windrose, quest items like this are indestructible and can stack up to 100, though you generally only need one to progress the main story arc in the Foothills region.

To begin, you must have cleared the introductory pirate encounters in the central hub. Once the quest is active, your first objective lies in the extreme western portion of the Foothills.

| Objective | Location | Primary Task |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pirate Camp Clearance** | Extreme West Foothills | Eliminate all hostile pirate NPCs |
| **Naval Engagement** | Eastern Sea | Hunt and sink "The Unsinkable" |
| **Ruins Investigation** | Northwestern Ruins | Locate the wax-stained desk |
| **Key Acquisition** | Northern Pirate Camp | Loot the dead pirate for the key |

> **Warning:** Attempting to reach the northern pirate camp without the quest active will result in the key not spawning. Ensure "Needle in a Haystack" is tracked in your log.

## Phase 1: The Battle Against The Unsinkable

Once you have cleared the western pirate camp, the game triggers a massive naval event. You must travel to the eastern side of the map to locate a notorious ship named "The Unsinkable." Despite its name, the ship has specific vulnerabilities that skilled captains can exploit.

Naval combat in Windrose during 2026 has been refined to emphasize positioning. To defeat The Unsinkable, you should maintain a circular path around the vessel. This prevents the enemy from lining up a full broadside shot against your hull.

### Ship Combat Stats

| Feature | The Unsinkable Stats | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hull Health** | High (Legendary Tier) | Use Heavy Cannonballs for maximum DPS |
| **Speed** | Moderate | Use Chain Shot to damage sails and slow it down |
| **Weaponry** | Dual-Side Cannons | Stay in the "blind spot" (diagonal to the bow) |
| **Crew Strength** | Elite Pirates | Prepare for a boarding action once health is low |

After you have shredded the ship's health, move in to board. Clearing the deck of the remaining pirates is necessary to progress. Once the ship is captured, your quest marker will update, pointing you toward the Northwestern Ruins.

## Phase 2: Navigating the Northwestern Ruins

The journey to the **windrose ancient temple key** continues on land. The northwestern portion of the Foothills is notorious for its pack of deadly wolves. These enemies move faster than standard pirates and can inflict a "Bleed" status effect that drains your health over time.

1.  **Dodge and Weave:** Do not engage every wolf pack. Use the terrain to your advantage and sprint toward the camp ruins.
2.  **Locate the Camp:** Look for a small cluster of tents near the crumbling stone pillars.
3.  **The Wax-Stained Desk:** Inside the main tent, you will find a wax-stained desk. Interact with this to find the clues leading to the key's final location.

## Phase 3: Obtaining the Windrose Ancient Temple Key

With the intelligence gathered from the desk, head to the northern side of the Foothills. You will encounter another pirate camp. Unlike the previous camps, this one is littered with the aftermath of a skirmish.

Follow the quest marker precisely to a specific dead pirate lying near the outskirts of the camp. Search his pockets to finally obtain the **windrose ancient temple key**. 

> **Tip:** If the quest marker seems bugged, try reloading your UI or checking if any remaining pirates in the camp need to be cleared first.

## Phase 4: Entering the Ancient Temple

Now that the **windrose ancient temple key** is in your possession, travel to the Ancient Ruins located in the heart of the Foothills. Look for the massive sealed stone door. Interacting with the mechanism will consume the key (or check for its presence) and open the path to the inner sanctum.

Inside, the atmosphere shifts. You are now entering a boss arena where you must face the legendary Israel Hands.

## Boss Guide: Israel Hands

Israel Hands is a high-mobility boss who specializes in rush attacks and projectile spam. This is a 1v1 encounter that tests your mastery of the Windrose parry system.

### Israel Hands Move Set

| Attack Name | Description | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shadow Rush** | A fast dash followed by a sword thrust | Parry just as his blade glints |
| **Flintlock Fan** | Fires three projectiles in a cone | Dodge-roll to the left or right |
| **Pirate's Fury** | A 4-hit melee combo | Block the first three, parry the fourth |
| **Smokescreen** | Disappears and reappears behind you | Listen for the audio cue and turn 180° |

The best time to strike Israel Hands is immediately after he finishes a combo. He has a brief recovery window where he is vulnerable to heavy attacks. If you successfully parry his Shadow Rush, he will be staggered, allowing for a critical hit.

## Rewards and Loot

*   **The Buccaneer's Bane:** A powerful legendary sword with high critical strike chance.
*   **Ancient Artifact:** A unique accessory that provides a 10% boost to naval combat damage.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the windrose ancient temple key before starting the quest?**

A: No. The key is a quest-specific item that only appears in the dead pirate's inventory once you have reached that specific stage of "Needle in a Haystack."

**Q: What happens if I lose the naval battle against The Unsinkable?**

A: If your ship is sunk, you will respawn at the nearest port. You can re-engage the ship by sailing back to the eastern side of the map. Your progress in the quest will be saved.

**Q: Is the Israel Hands fight mandatory for the main story?**

A: Yes, defeating Israel Hands and using the **windrose ancient temple key** is required to unlock the subsequent regions of the map and progress the 2026 campaign.

**Q: Can I use the key more than once?**

A: While the item stacks up to 100, a single key is typically consumed or used to permanently unlock the Ancient Temple gate for your character. You may need to acquire it again in New Game Plus or on different character files.

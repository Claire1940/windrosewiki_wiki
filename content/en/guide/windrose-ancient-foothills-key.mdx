## Step 1: Initiating the Needle in a Haystack Quest

To begin your hunt for the **windrose ancient foothills key**, you must first trigger the "Needle in a Haystack" quest. This quest acts as the narrative thread connecting several locations across the map.

Your first objective is located in the extreme western portion of the Foothills. You will encounter a heavily fortified pirate camp. To progress, you must clear out all hostile NPCs in this area. These pirates are known for their aggressive pack tactics, so bringing area-of-effect (AoE) abilities is highly recommended. Once the camp is cleared, a cinematic or quest update will trigger, signaling the start of the next phase: a naval confrontation.

> **Warning:** The pirates in the western camp scale with your level. Ensure your armor is fully repaired before engaging, as the final wave includes several elite marksmen.

## Step 2: The Battle with The Unsinkable

After clearing the western camp, the quest shifts focus to the eastern side of the map. You are tasked with hunting down a legendary vessel known as "The Unsinkable." This is not a standard ship encounter; it is a boss-level naval battle that requires precise maneuvering and heavy firepower.

### Naval Combat Tactics

To defeat The Unsinkable and move closer to the **windrose ancient foothills key**, follow these tactical recommendations:

| Tactic | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Movement** | Maintain a **circular path** around the enemy ship to stay out of their primary broadside arc. |
| **Ammunition** | Use **heavy cannonballs** to shred the hull health quickly once the shields are down. |
| **Defense** | Brace for impact when you see the red indicator on the water, signaling incoming mortar fire. |
| **Boarding** | Only attempt to board once the ship's health bar turns yellow. |

Once you have successfully boarded and cleared the crew of The Unsinkable, you will receive the next set of coordinates leading to the northwestern ruins.

## Step 3: Searching the Northwestern Ruins

With the naval battle won, head to the northwestern ruins. This area is infested with deadly wolves that can easily overwhelm a distracted player. These wolves have a high bleed proc rate, so keep some restorative items on your quick-bar.

Navigate through the ruins until you locate a small camp within the stone structures. Your goal here is to find the **wax-stained desk**. Interacting with this desk provides the final clue needed to pinpoint the exact location of the key. It reveals that the key was stolen by a pirate who fled toward the northern side of the Foothills.

## Step 4: Locating the Windrose Ancient Foothills Key

Follow the quest marker to the pirate camp situated on the northern edge of the Foothills. Unlike the previous camps, you aren't looking for a chest or a boss drop. Instead, you need to search the environment carefully.

The **windrose ancient foothills key** is located in the pocket of a dead pirate near the center of the camp. Search the bodies scattered around the tents until the interact prompt appears. Once you have the key, it will appear in your quest inventory, and you are ready to head to the Ancient Temple.

## Step 5: The Temple Gate and Israel Hands

The final destination for the **windrose ancient foothills key** is the temple gate found within the ancient ruins. This massive stone door is sealed by ancient magic that only the key can dispel. 

Upon using the key, the door will slide open, leading you into a 1v1 arena against the boss, **Israel Hands**. This encounter is a test of your mechanical skill and reaction time.

### Israel Hands Boss Mechanics

| Move Type | Description | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rush Attack** | A high-speed dash that ends in a heavy slash. | **Dodge sideways** at the last second or use a heavy parry. |
| **Projectiles** | Throws multiple knives in a fan pattern. | Block or hide behind the temple pillars. |
| **Combo Enders** | A slow, overhead strike after a 3-hit combo. | This is your **best damage window**; strike immediately after. |

> **💡 Tip:** Israel Hands is highly susceptible to parries. If you can time your parry against his rush attack, he will be staggered for 3 seconds, allowing for a full combo of heavy attacks.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the windrose ancient foothills key without the quest?**

A: No. The key is a quest-specific item. While you can visit the northern camp at any time, the dead pirate containing the key will not be interactable unless the "Needle in a Haystack" quest is active and you have reached the correct step.

**Q: Is the Israel Hands boss fight repeatable?**

A: Once you use the key to open the temple, the door remains open. However, Israel Hands is a unique quest boss. While you can revisit the temple, the primary legendary rewards are only granted upon the first successful defeat.

**Q: What happens if I lose the key?**

A: The **windrose ancient foothills key** is marked as an indestructible quest item. It cannot be sold, dropped, or destroyed. If you cannot find it in your inventory, check your "Quest Items" tab specifically, as it does not sit with regular loot.

**Q: Are there any level requirements for this quest?**

A: While there is no hard level cap, the naval battle against The Unsinkable and the fight with Israel Hands are tuned for players who have reached at least level 35. Attempting this earlier may result in significant difficulty due to the high damage output of the bosses.

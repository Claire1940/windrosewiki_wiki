Navigating the treacherous waters of Windrose requires more than just a basic raft; eventually, every captain needs to secure the **windrose frigate recipe** to command the seas effectively. This mid-game vessel offers a significant jump in durability and firepower, but obtaining the blueprint is tied to specific story milestones and boss encounters. To successfully unlock the **windrose frigate recipe**, players must progress through the main narrative, specifically the "Revenge Best Served Cold" questline, which serves as the primary gatekeeper for advanced naval engineering.

In this guide, we will break down the exact steps needed to acquire the materials, defeat the necessary bosses, and ultimately craft your Frigate. ## The Road to the Windrose Frigate Recipe

Before you can even look at the blueprints for a Frigate, you must establish a foundation of iron production. In Windrose, progression is strictly horizontal and vertical; you cannot simply buy your way into a top-tier ship without proving your mettle in combat and exploration. The recipe itself becomes available once you have gained access to the southern regions and cleared the prerequisite naval combat trials.

| Prerequisite | Requirement | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **Questline** | Revenge Best Served Cold | Must be active to enter boss arenas |
| **Key Item** | Black Marks | Collected through regional exploration |
| **Material Access** | Iron Ingots | Requires defeating Thomas Richards |
| **Crafting Level** | Level 5 Equipment | Highly recommended for the boss fight |

> **Warning:** Do not attempt to rush the Frigate build before upgrading your personal gear to at least Level 5. The enemies guarding the recipe components utilize input-reading mechanics that punish under-geared players.

## Unlocking Iron Smelting: Defeating Thomas Richards

A core component of the **windrose frigate recipe** is the use of high-grade Iron Ingots. While you can find Iron Ore early in your journey, the ability to smelt it is locked behind a boss fight with Thomas Richards. This encounter is famously difficult for those who rely solely on ranged combat or brute force.

### Boss Strategy: Thomas Richards

Thomas Richards is a tanky opponent who utilizes a mix of explosives and heavy melee combos. He is designed to counter "kiting" strategies, making the pistol a risky choice.

*   **Bomb Tossing:** When you see Richards winding up to throw bombs, perform two quick dodges backward. He can vary the spread of these explosives, so maintain a safe distance until the area is clear.
*   **Input Reading:** Richards will often dodge pistol shots the moment the trigger is pulled. It is more effective to use your firearm as a finishing tool rather than a primary damage source.
*   **The 2-Hit Rule:** The safest way to wear him down is to wait for his combo animation to finish, strike twice with a melee weapon, and immediately dodge out. Greed is the primary cause of failure in this fight.

## Essential Materials for the Frigate

Once iron smelting is unlocked, the actual gathering for the **windrose frigate recipe** begins. You will need a combination of refined metals, treated wood, and specialized naval components.

| Material | Quantity | Primary Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Iron Ingots** | 50 | Smelted Iron Ore (Post-Thomas Richards) |
| **Hardwood Planks** | 120 | Logging in the Southern Wilds |
| **Reinforced Sails** | 4 | Crafted at a Loom with Silk and Hemp |
| **Bronze Fasteners** | 30 | Found in sunken shipwrecks |

To gather these efficiently, focus on the "South" fast travel point discovered during the "Revenge Best Served Cold" quest. This area is rich in Iron Ore deposits that can be harvested once the boss is defeated.

## Character Build for Recipe Hunting

The journey to claim the **windrose frigate recipe** involves long stretches of exploration and high-intensity combat. Your character's stats should reflect this necessity. High endurance is not just for sprinting; it is vital for the dodge-heavy combat style required to beat the recipe's guardians.

### Recommended Stat Distribution

1.  **Endurance (12+):** This is the highest priority. A large stamina bar allows for the "hit-hit-dodge" rhythm essential for boss fights.
2.  **Temporal Health:** Investing in skills like "You Will Answer For This" provides temporal health when you take damage, allowing you to recover HP through aggressive counter-attacking.
3.  **Tankiness:** Perks like "Just a Flesh Wound" and "Stout Frame" ensure that a single mistake during a boss combo doesn't result in a wipe.

> **💡 Tip:** Max out the "Marathon Runner" skill early. The extra stamina recovery is a game-changer when navigating the large maps required to find the **windrose frigate recipe** components.

## Navigating the Questline: Revenge Best Served Cold

The **windrose frigate recipe** is not just found in a chest; it is earned through narrative progression. You must follow the quest log meticulously. The "Black Marks" mentioned in the quest log are essential for gaining entrance to the boss fight arena down south.

*   **Exploration:** Clear all the fog of war in the northern quadrants of the map first. This is where most Black Marks are hidden within pirate encampments.
*   **Signposting:** The game is generally well-signposted. If a door is locked or an arena is inaccessible, check your quest log to see if you have missed a specific map interaction or a required NPC conversation.
*   **Difficulty Scaling:** If you find yourself stuck on the requirements for the **windrose frigate recipe**, the game allows you to lower the difficulty. Because you cannot "over-level" via gear until after you unlock smelting, lowering the difficulty is a valid strategy for players struggling with the mechanical skill required for Thomas Richards.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the windrose frigate recipe specifically?**

A: The recipe is a reward for completing the "Revenge Best Served Cold" questline and defeating the boss Thomas Richards, who unlocks the prerequisite iron smelting ability.

**Q: Can I get the Frigate without fighting Thomas Richards?**

A: No. Iron Ingots are a mandatory requirement for the Frigate, and the ability to smelt them is strictly locked behind the Thomas Richards encounter.

**Q: What is the best weapon to use for the Frigate questline?**

A: While many players enjoy the pistol, it is difficult to use against the main bosses due to input reading. A fast melee weapon or a heavy two-handed weapon (if you are good at timing) is generally more effective for the hit-and-run tactics required.

**Q: Is there a way to farm Iron Ore before the boss fight?**

A: Yes, you can collect the ore from deposits in the southern region early, but you will not be able to turn them into ingots until the smelting recipe is unlocked via the boss fight.

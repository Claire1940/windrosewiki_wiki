## The Fundamentals of Cannon Upgrades

Before you can pursue legendary blueprints, you must master the basics of the Shipwright's Workshop. Every player begins with a standard 12-pounder, but this weapon can be significantly enhanced through direct upgrades. Upgrading your cannons increases their base damage and durability, allowing you to stay in the fight longer.

To upgrade your cannons, you will need a steady supply of Copper and Wooden Planks. While Copper is found in deposits across various islands, Wooden Planks must be crafted at your workbench using raw wood.

| Upgrade Level | Required Materials | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | Copper Ore, Wood | +10% Base Damage |
| **Tier 2** | Copper Ingots, Wooden Planks | +15% Reload Speed |
| **Tier 3** | Steel Nails, Hardwood | +20% Critical Chance |
| **Tier 4** | Foothills Iron, Reinforced Planks | Maximum Damage Output |

> **💡 Tip:** To farm wood efficiently, position your ship close to a dense forest on the shore and fire your cannons into the trees. This knocks them down instantly, allowing you to collect the wood with an axe without the tediousness of felling each tree manually.

## The "Perfectly Ordered" Meta

When looking for the **windrose best canons** in the early to mid-game, the **Perfectly Ordered 12-pound Roost** stands out as the premier choice. This weapon is not found through random loot but is earned through dedication to the Buccaneer’s Hideout.

The Roost's unique perk is a game-changer for naval skirmishes: hitting an enemy within 4 seconds of firing increases your reload speed by 30% for a duration of 22 seconds. This creates a "rhythm" of fire where, as long as you maintain accuracy, your DPS (damage per second) scales exponentially.

### How to Unlock the Roost
1.  **Locate the Bounty Agent:** Visit the Buccaneers' Hideout.
2.  **Earn Reputation:** Collect Deckhand Insignias by sinking enemy ships and killing Blackbeard’s deckhands.
3.  **Reach Rank 2:** Turn in your insignias to the Provisioner to reach the second reputation tier.
4.  **Purchase the Blueprint:** The Perfectly Ordered 12-pound Roost costs approximately 200 Pas p (the local currency).

## Faction Synergies and Naval Tactics

The power of the **windrose best canons** is amplified by the Naval Tactics books you can slot into your ship. These are primarily found in Tortuga, a hub for three major factions: The Brethren of the Coast, the People of Tortuga, and the Smugglers of Port Royal.

The Smugglers of Port Royal are particularly important for cannon-focused builds. At Reputation Level 3, they unlock advanced tactics that modify how your cannons interact with enemy hulls.

| Tactic Name | Effect | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Broken Rhythm** | Hitting an enemy reduces their reload speed and damage by 20% for 30s. | Defensive playstyles |
| **Ambush Volley** | If you haven't dealt or taken damage for 2m, the next volley deals +130% damage. | Stealth/First-strike builds |
| **Hull Repair** | Restore 3% ship health every 3s while out of combat. | Long-range kiting |
| **Filibuster Set** | Reduces attack stamina consumption by 20%. | Heavy broadside spamming |

## Expanding Your Arsenal: Ship Designs

Even the best cannons are limited by the ship carrying them. The default starting vessel has limited gun ports, which caps your total damage output regardless of your cannon quality. To truly utilize the **windrose best canons**, you must upgrade your ship design.

The Brethren of the Coast in Tortuga sell designs for the **Brig** and the **Frigate**. The Frigate, in particular, is a 36-gun powerhouse. Transitioning to a Frigate allows you to mount multiple "Roost" cannons, creating a broadside that can delete smaller ships in a single synchronized volley.

### Essential Ship Upgrades
*   **Master Bottle Maker:** Hire Rosalinda Merca (500 Pas p) to double your elixir production, ensuring you have enough "Dead Eye Grog" to boost cannon damage by 15%.
*   **Resource Recovery:** Hire Jasper Crow to gain a 30% chance to recover resources spent on expensive cannon upgrades.

## Advanced Combat Strategies

Dominating a 1v3 battle requires more than just pointing and clicking. You must manage your ship's positioning and stamina. If you are using the Filibuster set, your stamina consumption is lower, allowing for more frequent "dashes" with your ship to avoid incoming fire.

When engaging heavy convoys, use the "Broken Rhythm" tactic to debuff the largest ship in the fleet first. By reducing their damage and reload speed, you effectively take their biggest gun out of the fight, allowing you to focus on the smaller, faster escorts.

> **Warning:** Never ignore your gear's item level. Even with the best cannons, a low-level hull will crumble against Foothills-tier enemies. Ensure your average item level is at least 4 before venturing into the Smuggler's Archipelago.

## Resource Farming for Elite Cannons

To maintain and upgrade the **windrose best canons**, you will need vast amounts of rare materials. Beyond basic copper, you will eventually require Foothills Iron Ingots.

1.  **Fast Travel Points:** Build fast travel points on every island you visit. This allows you to cycle through copper and iron deposits as they respawn.
2.  **Ocean Loot:** Keep an eye out for colored lights in the water. 
    *   **White/Green:** Basic wood and repair kits.
    *   **Blue:** Steel nails, silver, and currency.
    *   **Yellow:** Epic blueprints and rare crafting components.
3.  **Contraband Trading:** Take your ancient chalices, wooden talismans, and silver spoons to the buyer in the Smuggler's Archipelago. This is the most efficient way to earn the Pas p required for high-tier cannon blueprints.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the windrose best canons for solo players?**

A: The Perfectly Ordered 12-pound Roost is widely considered the best for solo play due to its reload speed buff. It allows a single player to maintain a high volume of fire that mimics the output of a larger crew.

**Q: Where can I find the Frigate ship design?**

A: The Frigate design is sold by the Brethren of the Coast in Tortuga. You will need to raise your reputation with them by completing faction-specific quests and turning in contraband.

**Q: Do I need to upgrade my melee gear for naval battles?**

A: While naval battles primarily use cannons, you will frequently be boarded or need to fight bosses on land to unlock new areas. Keeping your Halberd and armor upgraded is essential for surviving the transitions between sea and land combat.

**Q: How do I increase my ship's stamina?**

A: Ship stamina is influenced by your character's Endurance stat and specific food buffs. Consuming Coconut Milk with Bananas provides a significant bonus to endurance, allowing for more frequent naval maneuvers during intense combat.

## Locating the Tortuga Market

The first step in your journey to acquire the frigate is finding the right vendor. Unlike standard ports, the design for this ship is held exclusively by NPCs at the Tortuga Market. This market is tucked away within the second biome of the game world. If you are navigating from the starting areas, you will typically find this biome located to the far left of your primary map interface.

Once you enter the second biome, keep a close eye on your navigation markers. The Tortuga Market is a bustling hub for high-tier naval trade. Upon arrival, you need to seek out two specific NPCs known as the "Person of the Coast." These individuals act as the gatekeepers for advanced ship designs. While they offer various items, the Frigate (or Fragata) is their most prized blueprint.

| Location Feature | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Biome** | Second Biome (Western Region) |
| **Primary Hub** | Tortuga Market |

> **💡 Tip:** Ensure your current ship is well-repaired before venturing into the second biome, as the enemy encounters in this region are significantly more difficult than those in the starting waters.

## Mastering the Trust System

Even after finding the NPCs, you cannot simply purchase the **windrose fragata** with standard currency. The Person of the Coast operates on a reputation-based system known as "Trust." To even see the option to learn the frigate design, you must reach the maximum reputation tier.

### Reaching Trust Level 4
The trust system is divided into four levels. To unlock the frigate, you must reach **Trust Level 4**. This requires a total of 3,000 reputation points. These points are earned by turning in specific items that prove your prowess in naval combat.

### How to Earn Reputation Points
The most effective way to gain trust is by destroying other ships throughout the world. When you sink an enemy vessel, they often drop specialized tokens or naval artifacts. Collecting these and returning them to the Person of the Coast at Tortuga Market will steadily increase your level.

| Trust Level | Points Required | Unlocks |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| Level 1 | 500 | Basic Supplies |
| Level 2 | 1,200 | Advanced Cannonballs |
| Level 3 | 2,200 | Specialized Ship Parts |
| **Level 4** | **3,000** | **Frigate (Fragata) Design** |

## Crafting the Windrose Fragata

Once you have reached the maximum trust level and spent your points to learn the design, the ship does not automatically appear in your inventory. In Windrose, "learning" a ship simply unlocks the ability to manufacture it. You must return to your home base to begin the construction process.

### Using the Wharf
Navigate to the Wharf building within your base. This is the only location where large-scale ship construction can occur. Within the Wharf menu, you will now see the Frigate listed among your available blueprints. Unlike smaller vessels, the **windrose fragata** requires a massive amount of raw materials and refined components.

### Necessary Materials
While the exact resource count can fluctuate based on game balance updates in 2026, you should prepare to gather the following categories of items:
1. **Refined Timber:** Hardwood harvested from the second and third biomes.
2. **Iron Plates:** Processed ore used for hull reinforcement.
3. **Heavy Canvas:** Required for the massive sail surface area of a frigate.
4. **Naval Components:** Specialized parts often found in wreckage or purchased from high-level vendors.

When compared to the starter ships, the Frigate offers unparalleled stats in three key areas: Hull Integrity, Broadside Firepower, and Cargo Capacity. While it may lack the nimble turning radius of a Sloop, its ability to soak up damage makes it the king of prolonged engagements.

For players looking to engage in high-stakes [Windrose naval combat](https://store.steampowered.com/search/?term=Windrose), the Frigate is non-negotiable. It allows you to challenge elite boss ships and guard convoys that would otherwise shred a smaller vessel in seconds.

## Advanced Progression Tips

After you have successfully built your frigate, the journey isn't over. You can further enhance the vessel by installing high-tier modules. Since you have already reached Trust Level 4 with the Person of the Coast, you should check their inventory for exclusive upgrades that are only compatible with Frigate-class hulls.

*   **Reinforced Masts:** Increases the speed of the frigate, compensating for its natural weight.
*   **Heavy Culverins:** Extends the range of your broadside attacks.
*   **Gilded Figureheads:** Provides minor buffs to crew morale and repair speed.

> **Warning:** Losing a Frigate in combat can be a devastating financial blow. Always ensure you have enough spare materials in your warehouse to facilitate quick repairs or a full rebuild if the worst should happen.

## Strategic Deployment of the Frigate

The **windrose fragata** excels in "tanking" damage for a fleet. If you are playing with friends or utilizing AI escorts, position your frigate at the front of the formation. Its heavy armor allows it to draw enemy fire while smaller, faster ships flank the opposition. In solo play, use the Frigate's superior range to pick off enemies before they can close the distance and exploit your slower turn speed.

By following this guide, you will transition from a mere sailor to a true captain of the seas. The grind for 3,000 trust points is long, but the reward is a vessel that defines power in the world of Windrose.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the Tortuga Market located?**

A: The Tortuga Market is found in the second biome. On the world map, this is generally the area to the left of the starting zone. Look for a concentrated hub of NPC activity near the coastlines of the larger islands in that region.

**Q: Can I buy the windrose fragata with gold?**

A: No, you cannot purchase the ship directly with gold. You must earn 3,000 Trust points by turning in items from destroyed ships and reach Trust Level 4 with the "Person of the Coast" NPCs to unlock the blueprint. Once unlocked, you still need to gather raw materials to build it at your Wharf.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up Trust?**

A: The fastest method is to farm enemy NPC ships in the second biome. These ships drop the high-value items needed for the Trust system. Focus on larger enemy vessels, as they tend to drop more reputation-boosting items than smaller scouts.

**Q: Why can't I see the Frigate in my ship menu after buying the design?**

A: You must physically build the ship at the Wharf in your base. Learning the design only adds it to your crafting list. Ensure you have the required materials (Timber, Iron, Canvas) and a free ship slot before attempting to construct the **windrose fragata**.

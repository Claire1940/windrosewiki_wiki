## Locating the Windrose Ancient Village

The primary challenge in finding the **windrose ancient village** stems from the game's procedural generation. No two maps are identical, meaning you cannot simply follow a set of coordinates provided by another player. Instead, you must master the art of archipelago navigation and icon identification.

The village is always situated within the Foothills Archipelago, typically located south of the initial starting zone. As you sail or trek through the islands, you must keep a close eye on your mini-map and world map icons. The village is specifically designated by a **Wheat Field icon** with a completion tracker of **0/1**. If you see this icon, you have successfully located the site of the ancient ritual.

## Essential Preparation: Items and Materials

Before you step foot into the **windrose ancient village**, you must ensure your inventory is prepared. The ritual at the ancient altar is not free, and arriving empty-handed will result in a wasted trip. There are two main components to the offering: raw materials you bring with you and specific items found only within the village ruins.

### Gathering the Offering
You will need to collect **20 pieces of Meat**. This can be acquired by hunting any of the local wildlife found throughout the Foothills or the starting islands. It is recommended to farm this meat before you set out on your search for the village, as you don't want to be distracted by hunting once you've found the location.

### Finding the Ritual Oils
Once you arrive at the village, you must scavenge for **three Ritual Oils**. These are not items you can craft or bring with you; they are hidden within the scattered buildings of the village itself.

1.  **Search Ancient Chests:** The oils are typically found inside Ancient Chests located out in the open near the village structures.
2.  **Inspect Main Buildings:** At least one oil is usually hidden inside a larger structure.
3.  **Use Verticality:** Some buildings are inaccessible from the ground level. Look for signs or debris that you can jump on to reach higher entry points.

## Completing the Ancient Altar Ritual

With the 20 pieces of meat in your bag and the three ritual oils collected from the village chests, you are ready to approach the Ancient Altar. The altar is the focal point of the **windrose ancient village** and is usually located in a central clearing or a prominent stone structure.

Follow these steps to complete the ritual:
1.  Approach the altar and interact with it to open the offering menu.
2.  Place the 20 pieces of meat and the 3 ritual oils into the designated slots.
3.  Confirm the offering to trigger the ritual animation.
4.  Collect the **Legendary Boar Whistle** from the altar.

Once the whistle is in your inventory, you must **right-click** the item to bind it to your character. This will allow you to summon Truffle, your permanent boar companion.

## Understanding Your New Companion: Truffle

The Legendary Boar Whistle is one of the most powerful utility items in the 2026 version of the game. Unlike consumable summoning items, the whistle has **unlimited uses**. The only restriction is a standard cooldown period between summons if Truffle is defeated in combat or dismissed.

### Why Truffle is Essential
Truffle acts as a "tank" pet, capable of drawing aggro from multiple enemies while you deal damage from a distance or reposition. This is particularly useful in the Foothills where enemy density can quickly overwhelm a solo player. 

> **Warning:** While Truffle is hardy, he is not invincible. Keep an eye on his health bar during boss encounters, as the whistle's cooldown can leave you vulnerable if he falls too early in a fight.

## Navigating the Foothills Archipelago

The journey to the **windrose ancient village** is often more dangerous than the ritual itself. The Foothills are home to aggressive fauna and environmental hazards that can end a run prematurely. To ensure you reach the village safely, consider the following tactical advice:

*   **Island Hopping:** Use a raft or small boat to move between islands rather than swimming, as aquatic predators are common in the deep channels.
*   **Stamina Management:** Climbing the rocky outcrops of the Foothills consumes significant stamina. Always carry stamina-restoring consumables.
*   **Weather Awareness:** Storms in the archipelago can reduce visibility, making it much harder to spot the Wheat Field icon on your map. It is often better to wait out a storm at a temporary camp.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I find the Windrose ancient village more than once?**

A: Each procedurally generated world contains at least one ancient village. While you can visit it multiple times, the Legendary Boar Whistle ritual can typically only be completed once per character to obtain the permanent pet.

**Q: What happens if Truffle dies in combat?**

A: If Truffle's health reaches zero, he will disappear. You simply need to wait for the whistle's cooldown to expire before you can summon him again. He does not require food or healing items to return at full strength.

**Q: I found a village but there's no Wheat Field icon. Is this the right place?**

A: No. There are various ruins and small settlements throughout the Foothills. The **windrose ancient village** specifically required for the Boar Whistle will always be marked with the Wheat Field (0/1) icon on your map.

**Q: Do I need to dig in the fertile soil to find the ritual oils?**

A: No, you do not need to dig. The ritual oils are located in ancient chests that are placed on the ground or inside buildings. Save your tools and time by focusing on the visible chests.

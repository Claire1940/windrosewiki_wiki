## Inventory Management and Storage Upgrades

Before you can focus on collecting **windrose new plans to learn in curios**, you must address your limited inventory space. You will quickly find that raw materials like stone and wood fill your pockets within minutes. To solve this, you need to prioritize crafting larger storage containers and better backpacks.

Nails are the "gatekeeper" resource for better storage. While you typically unlock nails through copper smelting, you can bypass this early by using an axe to break crates and barrels found in shipwrecks. These crates often drop enough nails to craft Large Chests, which offer significantly more slots than the basic starter baskets.

## The Fast Travel Bell Loop

Fast travel in Windrose is a tactical tool rather than just a convenience. Once you find the **windrose new plans to learn in curios** that allow you to craft or use Bells, your gameplay loop will change. You are limited to 10 personal travel points, so you must use them wisely.

The most efficient way to explore new islands is to carry a Bell in your inventory. When you reach a dense cluster of curios, place the Bell down to create a temporary anchor. You can then travel back to your base, empty your inventory, restock on food and potions, and instantly return to the Bell. Once you are finished with that area, pick the Bell back up to reclaim it for future use.

> **Warning:** You can fast travel directly from the helm of your ship or dinghy, but be aware that you cannot fast travel *back* to the ship unless you have placed a Bell on the deck.

## Base Comfort and the Rested Buff

Your base is more than just a place to store your **windrose new plans to learn in curios**; it is where you gain the vital "Rested" buff. This buff increases your stamina regeneration, which affects everything from sprinting to mining speed.

To increase the strength and duration of this buff, you must increase your base's Comfort level. However, simply placing ten chairs will not help. Comfort stacks based on the variety of decoration categories, not the quantity of items.

| Category | Example Items | Stacks With |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Seating** | Chair, Bench, Stool | No (Highest value applies) |
| **Surface** | Table, Desk, Counter | Yes |
| **Lighting** | Lantern, Chandelier, Torch | Yes |
| **Decorative** | Rug, Painting, Statue | Yes |

## Resource Gathering in Caves

Caves are the best locations to find mineral-related **windrose new plans to learn in curios**. To maximize your efficiency while mining, you should treat every cave as a mini-base. Before you start swinging your pickaxe, place a few storage baskets and torches near the entrance.

Mining generates a large amount of Stone alongside the valuable Ores. Instead of throwing the Stone away, store it in your entrance baskets. This allows you to carry only the high-value Ore back to your ship, while the Stone remains safely stored for when you eventually need it for base building. Since cave nodes replenish over time, having a pre-lit and organized cave makes future runs much faster.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the most windrose new plans to learn in curios?**

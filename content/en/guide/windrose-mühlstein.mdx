Whether you are looking to produce high-quality flour or process seeds for advanced recipes, the **windrose mühlstein** stands as a central pillar of your base's infrastructure. ## How to Unlock Farming in Windrose

Farming is a significant undertaking that is gated behind story progression. You cannot simply start planting seeds as soon as you wash ashore. You must first prove your strength by conquering the initial challenges of the island.

### Step 1: Conquer the Coastal Jungle
The journey begins in the Coastal Jungle. You must follow the main story quests until you face the region's primary boss. Defeating this boss is mandatory; while you can physically enter the next region, the Foothills, earlier, the game will not allow you to "discover" or unlock the farming recipes until the boss has been cleared.

### Step 2: Scouting the Foothills
Once the boss is defeated, head inland toward the Foothills. This region is characterized by its rugged terrain and ancient ruins. Because the world is procedurally generated, the exact coordinates of these ruins will vary for every player. Look for "Ancient Farms"—crumbling stone structures that indicate a history of agriculture.

### Step 3: Collecting Fertile Soil
Near these ancient farms, you will find small mounds of dark dirt. Use your pickaxe on these mounds to gather **Fertile Soil**. This is the core ingredient for crafting seed beds. You will need five units of Fertile Soil for every single seed bed you wish to place at your base.

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Progression** | Defeat Coastal Jungle Boss | Main Story |
| **Location** | Ancient Farms | Foothills Biome |
| **Tool** | Pickaxe (Any Tier) | Crafting Bench |
| **Resource** | Fertile Soil | Dirt Mounds in Ruins |

## Crafting and Utilizing the Windrose Mühlstein

The **windrose mühlstein** (or Millstone) is the primary station for grinding crops. Once you have harvested items like flax seeds or grains from your farm, the millstone processes them into refined goods. This is essential for high-tier cooking and crafting linen fabrics.

### Building the Millstone
After securing your farm, you should prioritize building the millstone near your storage containers. It requires stone and specialized materials found within the Foothills ruins. Once placed, you can interact with it to see the available grinding recipes.

> **Expert Tip:** Place your **windrose mühlstein** and your Spinning Wheel in the same crafting hub. Many recipes, such as those involving flax, require you to move between these two stations frequently.

## Setting Up Your Farm for Maximum Efficiency

When placing your seed beds, layout is everything. Because harvesting is a manual process that requires you to interact with every single plant, a spread-out farm will waste valuable time.

### Placement and Elevation
Avoid placing seed beds directly on uneven sand or terrain that might clip through the bottom. If a seed bed is partially covered by the environment, you may lose planting spots. It is best to build a raised wooden foundation and place your seed beds on top of it. This ensures a flat, clean surface for your crops.

### The 70-Minute Growth Cycle
All crops in the game follow a standardized growth cycle of approximately **70 minutes of real-time play**. While different plants may look different during their growth stages, they will all reach maturity at the same time if planted together.

| Crop Type | Growth Time | Primary Use | Seed Return |

| **Flax** | 70 Minutes | Linen Fabric / Ships | Low (Requires Foraging) |
| **Coco Plums** | 70 Minutes | Agility Food | Moderate |
| **Grains** | 70 Minutes | Flour / **Windrose Mühlstein** | High |
| **Fiber Plants** | 70 Minutes | String / Rope | Moderate |

## Priority Crops: The Flax Grind

If there is one resource you should focus on above all else, it is **Flax**. As you progress into the late game of 2026, the demand for Linen Fabric becomes astronomical. Some advanced ships and structures require upwards of 150 Linen Fabrics, and each fabric requires three pieces of Flax Fiber.

### Finding Flax Seeds
Flax is found exclusively in the Foothills. Look for clusters of purple flowers. Unlike some other crops, Flax does not always provide a net-positive seed return when harvested from a farm. This means you must constantly explore the Foothills to find more wild seeds to keep your farm running at full capacity.

### Processing via the Spinning Wheel and Millstone
Once harvested, take your Flax to the Spinning Wheel to create Linen. If you are producing oil or seed-based additives, the **windrose mühlstein** will be your next stop. Keeping a steady rotation of Flax in your seed beds is the only way to afford the high costs of late-game naval exploration.

> **Warning:** Do not confuse decorative plants like Palm Trees or Bananas with actual farming. These can be planted anywhere in the ground and do not require Fertile Soil or Seed Beds. Save your Fertile Soil for Flax and essential food crops.

## Optimizing Your Build with Food

The secondary purpose of your farm and the **windrose mühlstein** is food production. Depending on your character's build, you will need different buffs.

1.  **Agility Builds:** Focus on growing Coco Plums. These provide the necessary stamina and speed boosts required for hit-and-run combat styles.
2.  **Strength Builds:** Look for heavier grains and root vegetables that can be processed in the millstone to create hearty meals.
3.  **Exploration Builds:** Focus on high-duration foods that reduce hunger depletion, allowing you to stay in the Foothills longer without returning to base.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to find the windrose mühlstein blueprint?**

A: The blueprint for the **windrose mühlstein** is typically unlocked automatically once you collect your first batch of grain or specialized seeds in the Foothills after defeating the first boss. If it hasn't appeared, ensure you have built a basic Crafting Bench and have Stone in your inventory.

**Q: Does weather affect the growth of my crops?**

A: No, in the current 2026 version of the game, weather and location do not impact the 70-minute growth cycle. Whether you plant in the desert or the jungle, the timing remains the same. The only factor that matters is ensuring the seed bed is not obstructed by the ground.

**Q: How do I get more seeds if my farm isn't producing enough?**

A: Most crops in Windrose do not have a 1:1 seed return ratio. To maintain a large farm, you must periodically go on "foraging runs" in the Foothills. Look specifically for the purple flowers (Flax) and wild grain stalks to replenish your seed supply.

**Q: Can I automate the windrose mühlstein?**

A: Currently, processing at the millstone requires manual input of resources. However, you can stack multiple items in the queue. It is recommended to start a large batch of grinding before you head out on an exploration mission so the materials are ready when you return.

## Combat and Crew Management

Naval combat in Windrose is a thinking man's game. It isn't just about pointing your cannons and firing; you must account for the roll of the ship on the waves, the distance to the target, and the type of ammunition used. Chain shot can be used to disable enemy masts, while grapeshot is ideal for clearing the enemy deck before a boarding action.

Your crew is your most valuable resource. Each crew member has specific stats in areas like "Sailing," "Repair," and "Gunnery." As they gain experience, they will level up, allowing you to assign perks that further enhance your ship's performance. Keeping your crew's morale high is essential; a disgruntled crew may perform poorly in battle or, in extreme cases, attempt a mutiny.

> **💡 Tip:** Regularly visit taverns in port cities to recruit specialized crew members who might have unique "Veteran" traits.

## Combat and Character Progression

The ground combat in Windrose leans heavily into tactical precision rather than mindless clicking. With target locking, dodge rolls, and stamina management, it feels closer to an action RPG than a standard survival game. Your choice of weaponry matters because of stat scaling:

*   **Sabers:** Scale with Agility for fast, precise strikes.
*   **Clubs:** Scale with Strength for heavy, staggering blows.
*   **Off-hand Guns:** Can be aimed independently, allowing for hybrid combat styles.

## Naval Warfare and Crew Recruitment

A significant portion of the **windrose price** value comes from the naval simulation. Unlike games where ships are merely mobile bases, Windrose features active naval combat. You must manage your ship’s health, aim cannons, and eventually pull alongside enemy vessels for boarding actions.

The crew system adds a layer of settlement management. By rescuing crew members from pirate camps, you populate your base and your ship. As you progress, you can repair and captain larger vessels, moving from a humble raft to a formidable galleon.

## Exploration and Loot Rarity

The world of Windrose is a hybrid of procedural generation and handcrafted detail. While the islands themselves are generated to keep exploration fresh, the points of interest—such as ruins, caves, and cellars—are designed by hand. This ensures that when you follow a treasure map to an "X," the payoff feels earned.

Loot follows a traditional rarity scale, which encourages players to delve into the most dangerous dungeons.

1.  **Common (White):** Basic gear found early on.
2.  **Uncommon (Green):** Improved stats, found in basic camps.
3.  **Rare (Blue):** Found in dungeons; often includes unique modifiers.
4.  **Epic (Purple):** High-tier loot from bosses.
5.  **Legendary (Gold):** Can be "Ascended" to unlock additional supernatural abilities.

> **Warning:** Be wary of the day/night cycle. "Drowned" enemies crawl out of the water after dark and are significantly more dangerous than standard pirate foes. Ensure your base is fortified before nightfall.
